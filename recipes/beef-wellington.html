Beef Wellington

Description

Beef Wellington is dish that Gordon Ramsay makes. It looks nasty.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Mushrooms
  • Tarragon
  • Puff pastry

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Cook the mushrooms
  3. Cook the puff pastry
  4. Wrap the beef in the puff pastry
  5. Bake the beef
  6. Enjoy! Or don't.
